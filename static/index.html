Features

Built for your parish community

Everything your Social Communications Ministry needs to receive, manage, and display mass intentions — so every community prayer reaches the altar with dignity.

📋

Intention request management

Receive and organize parishioner requests in one dashboard. Encode names, categories, and date ranges — and every entry appears on the display instantly.

📺

Projector-ready display

A fullscreen display page that runs on any laptop, tablet, or phone pointed at a projector. Press F for fullscreen — it just works during every Mass.

📶

Works offline

If the internet drops during Mass, the screen keeps running from cached data. Names never disappear mid-celebration of the Eucharist.

⚡

Instant sync

When parish staff encodes a new intention, it appears on the display within seconds — no refresh needed. The community sees it together.

📅

Novena & multi-day intentions

Encode a name once for 9 days, 40 days, or any novena period. It appears automatically at every Mass within that range.

🎨

Parish theme colors

Customize the display screen and dashboard to match your parish colors — background, text, accent, and more.

👥

Multi-user access

Parish admin creates accounts for each staff member. Everyone logs in with their own credentials. All data stays within your parish.

🔤

Alphabetical display

Names are automatically sorted A to Z within each intention category — clean, consistent, and easy to read from the pews.

⛪

Multi-parish platform

Each parish has its own private space. Data is never shared between parishes. One platform, serving every parish that joins.

🔍

Parishioner name search

Parishioners can tap the search icon on the display to look up any name encoded this week — so they can confirm their loved one's intention before Mass begins.

✨

Smooth transitions

A cinematic wipe reveals each new category with the category name displayed for 5 seconds. Names fade in gently — dignified, never rushed.

💳

GCash & Maya payments

Parishioners submitting intentions can upload their GCash or Maya payment receipt directly from the registration portal for easy verification.

How it works

From request to the Eucharist — in minutes

Your parish office receives the intention, encodes it, and it appears at every Mass — united with the community's prayer.

1

Get an access code

Contact the Panalangin administrator to receive a one-time registration code for your parish.

2

Register your parish

Enter the code, your parish name, and an admin email. Your 3-month free trial starts immediately.

3

Offer the intention

Log in to the dashboard. Enter the offeror's name, choose the intention type, set the date range. One entry covers every Mass in that period.

4

Display at the Eucharist

Open the display URL on the projector laptop before Mass. Press F for fullscreen — the community sees every intention lifted at the altar.

5

Parishioners verify names

Anyone can tap the search icon on the display screen to confirm their loved one's name is included before the celebration begins.

Documentation

Everything you need to get started

  1. 1

    Register your parish

    Go to /register, enter your access code, parish name, and admin credentials. Your account is created instantly.

  2. 2

    Set up your display URL

    In Settings, choose a slug for your parish — e.g. sto-nino-cabuyao. Your display URL becomes /sto-nino-cabuyao/display. Bookmark it on the projector laptop.

  3. 3

    Add parish staff accounts

    In the Users tab, create login accounts for each staff member who will receive and encode mass intentions.

  4. 4

    Encode intentions daily

    In Add intention — enter the offeror name, pick a category, set dates, add all names for that offeror, and save. They appear on the altar screen instantly.

  5. 5

    Display at every Mass

    Open the display URL on the projector laptop before Mass. Press F for fullscreen. Leave it running — it syncs automatically and works even without internet.

  6. 6

    Parishioners can search names

    Tap the ⚲ search icon in the corner of the display screen. Type any name — results appear instantly, searching all intentions active this week.
